step 1: pour the dosa batter in a vessel
step 2: make the pan ready
step 3: pour one teaspoon of oil on the pan and spread it (it should be very less in quantity)
step 4: now take a small round bowl , dip it in batter and fill it
step 5: pour the batter taken in bowl on the pan, spread it on the pan in a circular way
step 6: let the batter heat for sometime and solidify 
step 7: now slowly start from the edges and turn the dosa and bring it out
step 8: serve it on a plate and the dosa is now prepared
